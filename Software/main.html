Undergraduate

At the University of Bradford, a degree is more than a qualification

Student life at Bradford is an adventure to be shared with new friends, on campus, in the city, or even studying abroad. We will help you enter the world of work fluent in a multitude of cultures, technology-savvy and primed to make a contribution in your chosen profession. As one of our students, you won’t just graduate – you’ll grow, develop and flourish and learn how to be a global citizen. Our courses are sculpted with industry input from many of the employers who will be sifting through your CV. You’ll be taught by the academic experts whose pioneering research is shaping the very future of those industries. Put your theory into practice in our world-class facilities and then on a work placement with some of the world’s biggest names.
